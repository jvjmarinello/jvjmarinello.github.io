MY JOURNEY

My name is Jeremy and I live in beautiful Denver, CO. I am passionate about web development and the ability to turn any idea into a software solution that could impact millions of people.

My background is atypical for a software engineer. Even though I took various engineering and science classes in College, I decided to start my career in corporate finance. Although this experience was instrumental in my development, I quit after 3 years to pursue my dream to become a software engineer.

Fast-forward to today, I've been working in the software industry for 9 years. As a Technical Program Manager I lead multiple software implementation projects overseeing the entire development lifecycle from planning to design and delivery.

I have spent the past 2 years polishing my coding skills and studying modern web development frameworks in order to build innovative applications on my own. I did this while working full-time and using resources online, mostly for free.

In my spare time, I love running up mountains, filming short documentaries and exploring new places. I hope you enjoy my personal website as much as I enjoyed building it.
